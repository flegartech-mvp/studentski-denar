## LinkedIn (2-3 short paragraphs, professional but not dry)

Slovenian students have one of the most tracked stipends in Europe — but almost no tools built specifically for them. So I built one.

Študentski Denar is a browser-first budgeting app: income tracking, expense categories, monthly summaries, spending analytics. Zero signup. Zero cloud. Your data never leaves your device. It runs on React 19, Vite, and Tailwind CSS, deploys to Cloudflare Pages, and passes Playwright tests.

The goal was simple: a tool that respects student privacy and just works. No paywalls, no email harvesting, no third-party APIs. If this is useful to you, the repo is public — feedback welcome.
